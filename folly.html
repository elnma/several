Folly for two

Studio: Introductory design
Instructor: Benjamin Freyinger
Fall 2019

A folly comprised of two doppelgängers and
an egg crate shell. The form of the doppel-
gängers is derived from a rectilinear bar
primitive which undergoes a deformation
via a guillotine-style jig.
