Substation 46

Studio: Building design
Instructor: Jason Payne
Winter 2020

A proposal for a substation and learning
center for the Los Angeles Department of
Water and Power (LADWP). An oversized
steel scaffold frame acts as the circulatory
apparatus, which programmatic elements are
slotted into. Three tensile membrane "tents"
are housed within the steel frame, providing
cover for the electrical equipment and
workers below.
