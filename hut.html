Prismatic hut

Studio: Comprehensive
Instructor: Kevin Daly
Partner: Gibson Bastar
Winter 2020

A proposal for a substation and learning
center for the Los Angeles Department of
Water and Power (LADWP). An oversized
steel scaffold acts as the primary circulatory
device, which programmatic elements are
slotted into. Three tensile membrane "tents"
are housed within the steel frame, providing
cover for the electrical equipment and workers.
